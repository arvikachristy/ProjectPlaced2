Overview



The platform being developed by Team 18 is a collaboration with a startup, Project Placed. The platform is a robust system with a range of capabilities for managing, tracking and improving communication within team projects.







Our Aim



Our aim is to make communication between students and their clients easier and to aid supervisors and teaching assistants with the tracking of this communication. We are hoping that this platform not only has a great benefit locally within UCL, but that it can also an effective tool used by universities nationwide.




Our Client



Project Placed is a centralised software platform and service that allows 100’s of Universities to efficiently connect to, and manage, the placement of students across 1,000’s of small and large companies to complete vocational projects that count towards their academic grade. Students will then begin to build a Placed Score that incorporates both their academic grades and vocational experience, and which is validated by universities and employers. This will help transform the static diploma into a richer, updateable, more connected record of a person’s skills, expertise, and experience.

Main Research



We are creating our own research in a small group sample size from student to student. We focused on the research group within UCL particularly Computer Science where we have collected results from more than 56% respondent from 2nd year Computer Science students. Questionnaire was distributed and we build our platform based on this result in order to increase satisfaction level during project placement particularly in communication between students and supervisors. We took all of the suggestions into consideration and design our platform around this research.
